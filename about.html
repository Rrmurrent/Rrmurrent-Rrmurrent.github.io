Angela Current

Angela Current is currently a student at the University of Minnesota. She is studying web developement in the hopes of someday being able to apply her newfound skills out in the world. In her free time Angela enjoys going on long hikes with her puppy. She is still brand new to the web developement world, but aspires to someday have a better idea of what she is doing.


Angela has been attending the coding bootcamp at the University of Minnesota for the last two and a half months. She began the program as a complete novice, unfamiliar with what a div was, let alone how to use it.


In the short time that Angela has been attending class she has learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, and is currently learning Node. Though it can be frustrating sometimes, Angela persists.
